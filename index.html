"Attack on Titans Mythbusters"

What is the Titans' origin story?

Titans originated from Ymir Fritz, who gained power from a spine-like Hallucigenia creature and passed it to her descendants.

Where are the Armin's parents?

Armin's parents were killed by the Military Police while attempting to leave the Walls using a hot air balloon.

What are the Zeke's true power?

Zeke's true power is controlling Pure Titans using his royal blood and scream.

What is the real intentions of Mikasa and her mother's kidnappers?

The kidnappers intended to sell Mikasa and her mother as slaves due to their rare Asian heritage.

What happened to the couple who used hot balloon to escape the walls?

They were executed by the Military Police to keep the truth about the outside world hidden.

What are the reason behind the Akerman's power awakening moments?

Ackerman power awakens when they experience intense survival stress, triggering their latent combat instincts and memories.

The Canned food mystery?

The canned food found by Sasha and Connie was from Marley, revealing the outside world's advanced civilization and language.
